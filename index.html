Our 10 Step Project Management Process

At Gabekile Projects, our systematic process gives clients confidence from concept to completion. Each stage is built on precision, accountability and multi-disciplinary expertise.

  • 1. Concept & Feasibility

    We begin with a detailed review of your needs and conduct site surveys to define a cost-effective, fit-for-purpose solution for your General Building or Civil requirements.

  • 2. Multi-Disciplinary Design

    Our in-house team creates integrated Civil, Structural, Mechanical, and Electrical designs, ensuring compliance, practicality and seamless construction from the start.

  • 3. Costing & Budget Review

    We align specifications and requirements with a detailed budget to ensure a cost-effective solution. This includes a thorough Quote Review and Quote Acceptance stage.

  • 4. Contract Finalisation

    Once the quote is accepted, we finalize the contractual agreement and assign a dedicated Project Manager to provide robust control over scope, schedule, and budget.

  • 5. Procurement & Logistics

    We source all materials and secure necessary subcontractors, implementing strict vetting and quality control to ensure supplies meet project standards and optimize supply chain efficiency.

  • 6. Civil & Structural Erection

    Execution of civil works, foundations, earthworks and the main building structure under stringent site safety protocols and continuous quality assurance checks.

  • 7. MEP Systems Installation

    Installation and connection of all Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing (MEP) systems, seamlessly integrating them with the main building infrastructure.

  • 8. Finishing & Close-Out

    Application of final finishes, painting, cladding and detailing to bring the project to its aesthetic and functional completion as per the approved design.

  • 9. Quality Assurance & Commissioning

    Thorough testing and commissioning of all systems to confirm full operational capacity, safety compliance and adherence to all regulatory standards.

  • 10. Client Handover & Training

    Formal project handover, delivery of final documentation (as-builts, warranties, manuals), and essential training for the client's operations and maintenance teams.
