Our History

"Building is more than just construction; it's about laying the foundation for future generations." — Keith Mbatsane, CEO

Gabekile Projects was established in 2018 by Mr. Keith Mbatsane, a seasoned Civil: Structural Engineer. From its inception, the vision was clear: to create a construction and engineering firm known for its reliability, quality, and commitment to community development in Southern Africa.

Starting with a strong foundation in general building, the company quickly expanded its expertise into vital areas of civil, electrical, mechanical engineering and project management. Each project undertaken has contributed to our reputation for delivering exceptional results on time and within budget, adhering to the highest industry standards.

Today, Gabekile Projects continues to grow, adapting to market challenges while maintaining our core values. We are proud of our journey and committed to building a lasting legacy of excellence.
